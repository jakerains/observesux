## DuBois, Nicole

- Departments:Legal
- Divisions:Legal
- Title:City Attorney
- Phone:(712) 279-6318
- Fax:(712) 224-5203
- Email:Email

Nicole manages the legal affairs and performs legal services for the City which includes preparation of resolutions, ordinances, contracts, leases, franchises and other legal documents for Council meetings. In addition, Nicole reviews ordinances and prepares City contracts, attends meetings of the City Council and City boards, commissions and committees. Nicole serves as legal advisor to the City Council, City Manager, City boards, commissions and committees and other officials of the City and represents the City in legal matters. Nicole brings suits as necessary and defends the City in suits brought against it. Nicole hires and provides direction to legal staff and determines and implements departmental goals including the development of office policies and procedures.
