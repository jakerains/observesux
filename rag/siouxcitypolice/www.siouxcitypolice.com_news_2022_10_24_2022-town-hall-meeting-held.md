# [2022 Town Hall Meeting Held](https://www.siouxcitypolice.com/news/2022/10/24/2022-town-hall-meeting-held)

## October 24, 2022 [Jeremy McClure](https://www.siouxcitypolice.com/news?author=5c6dc3cedb792698ae90a839)

We held our annual Town Hall Meeting for 2022 on Monday Oct. 24 at the Sioux City Public Museum.

We discussed the Strategic Plan for the City and the department, the Downtown Partners Crime Prevention Initiative, and the Neighborhood Network.

You can watch the recorded live stream at [https://fb.watch/gm\_aEKdfPQ/](https://fb.watch/gm_aEKdfPQ/)

Give us your thoughts at [www.siouxcitypolice.com/town-hall](https://www.siouxcitypolice.com/town-hall)

And you can download a copy of the slide and handouts by clicking the button below.
